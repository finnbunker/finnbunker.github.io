About Noah's Ark

We are a family run service providing professional day care, boarding and walking services for your furry family members.

We operate from home, with a separate annex and designated garden for hanging out, playing, eating and a dog nap when needed!

Having grown up around dogs and having some of my own I know how loved they are, and I am very conscious of how important they are to you and will always be aware of the massive trust you have put in to me to make sure they are happy, healthy and safe.

Before looking after your dog you will have an appointment with me so I can learn and understand both your (the owner) and the dogs needs, which will be met to a high standard. Each dog gets tailored care and fun. Boarding dogs are treated like a member of the family.

I am fully insured, and also licenced by West Berkshire Council (licence number 27544)

Where we will hang out

During the day for both boarding and daycare we will spend most of the day in between walks hanging out in the designated annex room and garden. There will be plenty to do with a large selection of toys to chew and chase, and plenty of varied beds to choose from for the all important naps and rest time.

The annex is fully air conditioned for the hotter months, and fully heated for the colder ones. The garden laid to astroturf is shaded by sun awnings when needed too.

Our location

We are lucky enough to be right on the edge of Wilders Folly, which leads to Sulham woods and beyond. So if we are going for a local walk there's plenty of opportunity to keep our adventures and exercise varied and interesting for everyone.

We will also go further afield for new adventures in my fully air conditioned van, with plenty of space for everyone.

Everyone will get a good clean and dry if there are puddles involved!
